## Shared visual direction

- Authentic Nigerian ingredients, colour, texture, and serving style.
- Premium natural editorial food photography suitable for recipe cards and detail pages.
- Landscape composition with the whole dish clearly visible.
- Warm neutral tabletop, soft daylight, restrained props, and no distracting clutter.
- No people, hands, text, labels, logos, watermarks, or fusion reinterpretations.
