For a Nigerian pantry app, there are actually two different ways to calculate recipe cost, and I recommend using the second one.
Option 1: Static market prices (not recommended)
You assign fixed prices like:
Rice (1 cup) = ₦600
Chicken = ₦900
This quickly becomes inaccurate because prices vary by:
City (Lagos vs Abuja vs Kano)
Store (Market vs Shoprite vs local supermarket)
Time (food inflation)
Option 2: Calculate from what the user actually bought (recommended)
Your app already records grocery purchases. Use those purchase prices instead of market estimates.
Example:
The user buys:
Item	Quantity Bought	Amount Paid
Rice	5 kg	₦12,500
Vegetable oil	3 L	₦9,600

100 g pepper

Oil	₦800
Chicken	₦7,000
Pepper	₦250
Total	₦9,300
